# 🚀 Sales & Demo Script: "The 30-Second Close"

This script is designed for a live demonstration in a restaurant. Your goal is not to show "features," but to show **speed**, **reliability**, and **revenue collection**.

## 📍 Preparation
1. Open the system on a tablet or laptop.
2. Go to the **Tables** module.
3. Ensure no active orders are on **Table #4**.

---

## 🎭 The Demo (30 Seconds)

### 00:00 - 00:05 | The "Zero Error" Entry
> *"Watch how fast we handle a walk-in. Table 4 is free. One tap, and I'm in."*
- **Action**: Tap **Table #4**. 
- **Action**: Go to **Menu**, tap 2-3 items quickly.
- **Value**: Visual speed and simplicity.

### 00:05 - 00:15 | The "Smart Kitchen"
> *"Now, I send this to the kitchen. No more handwritten tickets or lost slips."*
- **Action**: Tap **🍳 Send to Kitchen**.
- **Action**: Switch to **Kitchen** tab. Show the order in "Preparing" with the timer running.
- **Action**: Tap **Mark as Ready**.
- **Value**: Shows the order loop and prevents "chef-waiter" miscommunication.

### 00:15 - 00:30 | The "Kaspi Cash-out"
> *"The customer is ready to pay. They want Kaspi. I go back to the table, and here's the QR."*
- **Action**: Go to **Tables** (it's now Blue/Ready). Tap **Table #4**.
- **Action**: Tap **📱 Kaspi**.
- **Action**: Show the **Kaspi Modal** (The "Wow" moment).
- **Action**: Tap **Confirm Payment Received** -> **Yes, Confirm**.
- **Value**: Shows the professional collection flow and how the table is automatically freed for the next customer.

---

## 💰 The One-Sentence Pitch
> **"This system eliminates miscommunication with your kitchen and makes Kaspi payments 100% error-proof—so you never miss a Tenge."**

---

## 🛠️ Technical Sales Highlights (For the Tech-Savvy Owner)
- **Cart Locking**: Once an order is sent to the kitchen, it's locked. No accidental deletions.
- **Payment Log**: Every Kaspi transaction is recorded with a timestamp and Order ID for end-of-day reconciliation.
- **Double Confirmation**: The "Confirm" button requires two taps to ensure the waiter actually saw the money in their app.
